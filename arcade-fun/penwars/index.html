    HOW TO PLAY

    🎯 Goal: knock every other pen off the bench. Last pen standing wins the round!

    👆 Flick: press on your own pen, drag backwards like a slingshot, and release.

    • 🔄 Players take turns in order — one flick each.
    • ⚖️ All pens stay on the bench the whole time — it's a free-for-all!
    • 🎯 Aim at any opponent's pen. Chain reactions are the best!
    • 🌪️ Watch out — your own pen can fly off if you aim badly.
    • 💛 Yellow dots preview your path. Red ring = dangerously close to the edge.
    • 👑 Win enough rounds to claim the match — check the scoreboard!

    ⌨️ P or Esc to pause mid-match.
