    HOW TO PLAY

    ◀ ▶ / A D — switch lanes  •  SPACE — activate ability  •  P / ESC — pause

    📱 On touch screens: tap left / right side to move, tap center to use your ability.

    • ◯ Rings — collect for score, EXP and combo.
    • ▲ Hazards — avoid! One hit destroys your ship.
    • 🛡 Shield — blocks one hit. 🐌 Slow — slows time. 🧲 Magnet — attracts rings.
    • ⚡ Ability — fills with EXP. Ghost = invulnerable. Blast = clears hazards.
    • 🌊 Waves — every wave is faster and meaner. Survive!
